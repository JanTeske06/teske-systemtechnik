## How a booking works

You choose a free slot in the calendar, pay securely online via Stripe, and immediately receive a confirmation with the Google Meet link for the call. The whole process takes less than two minutes, and the appointment is set.

## Two formats to choose from

The online consultation comes in two lengths:

- **30 minutes for €75**, ideal for a first, focused assessment
- **60 minutes for €150**, when we go deeper into architecture, feasibility or concrete implementation

Both prices include VAT.

## When a call is worth it

A consultation is the right entry point when you have an automation or software project in mind and want a sound, honest assessment: what is feasible, roughly what it costs, and what a sensible first step looks like. If you then book a project, the price of the call is credited against it.
